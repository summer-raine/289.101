About

Hogwarts School of witchcraft and wizardry is a school for talented young wizards and witches. Hogwarts dates back to the Tenth Century and was founded in the Scottish Highlands by our four great founders and house namesakes - Godrick Gryffindor - Salazar Slytherin - Rowena Ravenclaw and Helga Hufflepuff. The castle is a stronghold for ancient magic and is completely invisible to the muggle eye.

Rules

I - The forest on the grounds is forbidden to all pupils. II - There is to be no use of Auto-answering quills for any assignments or exams. III - There must be no use of magic on eachother in the hallways or library. IV - All students must be in bed by 10pm and must not be found wandering the halls past said time. V - Absolutely no swimming in the black lake so as to avoid annoying al inhabitants.
VI - The resticted section in the library is off limits to students unless they present a signed note by parent or teacher. VII - Students shall be in uniform at all times while attending and in between classes. VIII - Students shall maintain decorum and behaviour at all times. IX - Alcohol and tobacco are strictly prohibited on Hogwarts grounds at all times. X - Studensts must not leave school grounds unless permitted by head master or collected by parent or guardian.
